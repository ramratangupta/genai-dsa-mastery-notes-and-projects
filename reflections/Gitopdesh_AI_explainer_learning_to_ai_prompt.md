## **4. Learning About MCQs**

MCQs were the hardest problem.

Even with instructions like:

* ‚ÄúPut options on new lines‚Äù
* ‚ÄúUse A, B, C, D format‚Äù

The model still printed:

‚ÄúA. B. C. D.‚Äù on the **same line**.

I was completely stuck.

Then I learned:

> **LLMs compress lines in non-English languages.**

The solution was:

* Strict MCQ format
* Blank lines
* Self-correction rules
* Mandatory newlines after every option

Suddenly‚Ä¶

### **MCQs became perfect.**

A major breakthrough.
